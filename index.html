TAKE IT AND MAKE IT

Projects you know and love, remixed for 21st century learning.

Take It and Make It by Chloe Taylor Tech is project dedicated to "old school" elementary school projects. It invites the reader to take their favorite projects and make them their own. This site is for parents and educators, it is a remix of traditional elementary school activities with a technological twist. Take It and Make It gives parents and educators an easy entry into STEM education by building on projects they know and love. I was inspired to create this site through my coursework with Project Code from New York Public Library, which introduced new concepts in front- end web development to me in a structured but challenging way.
